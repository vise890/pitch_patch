Your Questions Answered!

What is a Pitch?
A pitch is a 90 second video and short text description of an idea that you are looking for help to turn into reality.
Why would I want to do a Pitch?
  • To find other people who are just as excited about your ideas as you are.
  • To receive valuable feedback on your idea.
  • To quickly and easily validate your idea
Great, so how do I add my Pitch to PitchPatch?
Add your pitch here! Its easy.
What if I don't want to share my idea with EVERYONE? Can I have a Pitch that only selected people can view?
A "public pitch" appears on our website allowing for anyone to search for and connect with.
If you prefer to keep your pitch private, we'll instead give you a personal URL, shareable with whomever you choose. Your picth will not be published to our website and people will not be able to search or view it (without your private URL).
